Your true artificial intelligence consultant
We are an Artificial Intelligence service company created in 2019 with HQ in Kyiv, Ukraine. Main area of expertise and activity is Computer Vision with a variety of specific applications from Autonomous Driving and Robotics to Security.
What we do

  • Machine vision for Industry
     Automated or semi-automated solutions with robotic arms or industrial robots moving in a closed environment. Conditions vary from artificially prepared to common industrial.

  • Object Detection
     Object movement and scene placement detection. Posture and movement reconstruction, facial and whole body recognition. Physical models for the objects detected with rigid movement frameworks.

  • Dataset Processing
    Dataset analysis, filtering and labelling for existing models and in interaction with new model setup

  • Visual Odometry and SLAM
    Reconstruction of movement in closed or open world environment with retina, depth and LIDAR based sensor systems. Sensor simulation and environmental modeling (LIDAR ray casting, RGB/ToF/IR effects simulation)

  • Deployment and Integration
    Integration of AI and ML solutions into existing operational schemes ranging from small setups to big data analysis centers

  • AI research
     Research services in computer vision and generic ML areas
